Admissions

A warm and simple admission journey for families.

For the client demo, this page presents a friendly enquiry flow for parents exploring play school, pre-primary, and primary admissions.

Admission process

  1. Submit an enquiry with child and parent details.
  2. Visit the campus and explore classrooms and play spaces.
  3. Share basic documents and age or class information.
  4. Confirm admission and complete the fee process.
